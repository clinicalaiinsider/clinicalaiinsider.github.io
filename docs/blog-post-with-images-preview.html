What is ATLAS?

ATLAS is the flagship web application of the OHDSI ecosystem. It provides a graphical interface for vocabulary exploration, cohort definition, characterization, estimation, prediction, and pathway analysis.

The ATLAS home screen provides navigation to all OHDSI analytical tools

Why Cohorts Are the Foundation

In clinical research, a cohort is a defined group of patients who share specific characteristics. Unlike ad-hoc database queries, cohorts are reproducible, shareable, standardized, versioned, and portable across 450+ OMOP databases.
